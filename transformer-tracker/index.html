Supply Chain Constraints

Key bottlenecks limiting transformer production capacity

Critical Materials

Grain-Oriented Electrical Steel (GOES)
Specialized steel for transformer cores. US 70% import dependent. Section 232 tariffs apply.
High-Voltage Bushings
Insulated conductors through transformer tanks. 60% imported. Few qualified suppliers globally.
Copper (Winding Grade)
Price volatility impacts costs. Competition from EV/renewable sectors.
Transformer Oil
Mineral insulating oil. Adequate supply but environmental regs increasing.

Capacity Constraints

Manufacturing Facilities
Only 6 US facilities can produce LPTs. New facility requires $100M+ and 2+ years.
Skilled Labor
2-5 year training time. 30% of workforce retiring in next decade.
High-Power Testing
Only ~10 North American test facilities. Each test takes 2-4 weeks.
Transportation
LPTs require specialized heavy-haul. Limited rail car availability.
