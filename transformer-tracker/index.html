Critical Supply Chain Constraints

Key bottlenecks limiting production capacity

GOES Steel
Grain-oriented electrical steel for transformer cores. US 70% import dependent. Only one US producer.
Manufacturing Capacity
Only 6 US facilities can produce large power transformers. New facilities require $100M+ and 2+ years.
HV Bushings
Critical transformer component. ~60% imported. Limited qualified global suppliers.
Skilled Labor
2-5 year training time for transformer manufacturing. 30% of workforce retiring in next decade.
Copper Supply
Competition from EV and renewable sectors driving prices up. Potential supply gap by 2030.
