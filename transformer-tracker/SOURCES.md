## Methodology

### Lead Time Estimates
Lead times are compiled from multiple sources and represent estimated ranges:

1. **Utility IRP filings** - Often cite specific lead times in resource planning
2. **Industry surveys** - Periodic surveys by trade publications
3. **Manufacturer communications** - Direct quotes from sales/engineering
4. **Conference presentations** - Industry events (IEEE PES, DistribuTECH)
5. **Analyst reports** - Investment bank coverage

**Important caveats:**
- Lead times vary significantly by specification, complexity, and relationship
- Manufacturer backlogs change frequently
- Quoted lead times may exclude engineering/approval phases
- Emergency/expedite orders may differ from standard lead times

### Market Share Estimates
Market share figures are approximate, derived from:
- Manufacturer annual reports (where disclosed)
- Industry analyst estimates
- Trade publication surveys
- Installed base analysis

Market shares vary by:
- Geographic region
- Product segment (LPT vs distribution)
- Voltage class
- Application (utility vs industrial)

### Manufacturer Facility Information
Facility data gathered from:
- Company websites and press releases
- Industry directories
- News articles on expansions/investments
- SEC/investor filings

Capacity figures are estimates where not publicly disclosed.

### Materials Supply Chain
Raw materials data from:
- Trade associations (World Steel Association, ICSG)
- Producer annual reports
- Government trade data
- Commodity analysts

## Disclaimer

This database is provided for informational purposes only. The information is compiled from public sources and estimates. Users should verify critical data points directly with manufacturers and consult with procurement professionals before making business decisions.

**Not for use in:** Regulatory filings, contract negotiations, or legal proceedings without independent verification.
